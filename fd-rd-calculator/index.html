About FD & RD Calculator

Fixed Deposit (FD): A lump-sum investment made for a fixed period. Interest is calculated on the principal and paid at maturity.

Recurring Deposit (RD): Regular monthly deposits made for a fixed period. Interest is compounded and added to the principal.

Key Features:

  • Multiple compounding frequencies (Annually, Semi-Annually, Quarterly, Monthly, Daily)
  • Tax deduction (TDS) calculation
  • Real value after inflation
  • Effective annual return calculation
  • Detailed year/month-by-month schedules
  • Works for all Indian banks and financial institutions

Supported Institutions: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis Bank, PNB, BOI, and all other banks offering FD/RD.
