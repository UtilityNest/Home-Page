Retirement Date Calculator

Retirement Date Calculator helps government and private employees calculate their exact retirement date based on date of birth and retirement age.

How Retirement Date Is Calculated

The tool adds the selected retirement age to your date of birth and calculates the last working day as per standard retirement rules.

Why Use This Retirement Calculator?

  • Accurate retirement date
  • Supports Indian retirement rules
  • Helpful for pension planning

Who Should Use This Tool?

Government employees, PSU workers, and HR professionals can use this tool.

Find your official retirement date based on your date of birth and applicable retirement age.
